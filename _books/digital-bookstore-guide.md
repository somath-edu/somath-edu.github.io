## Overview

This guide explores the synergy between Jekyll's static site generation and NotebookLM's intelligent content analysis. Learn how to transform a simple blog into a rich, AI-powered digital storefront.

### Why Jekyll?

Jekyll provides a robust foundation for content-focused websites. By using collections, we can easily manage books as structured data objects.

### NotebookLM: Your AI Research Partner

NotebookLM allows you to:
- Generate deep-dive audio overviews.
- Create study guides for complex topics.
- Answer user questions based on your book's content.
